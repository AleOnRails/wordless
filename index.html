Stop writing Wordpress themes like it's 1998.

Wordless is an opinionated Wordpress plugin that dramatically speeds up and enhances your custom theme creation.

Alright, what does it offer?

  • A structured and clean theme organization
  • Haml views
  • Sass & Compass stylesheets
  • Coffeescript logic
  • 40+ Rails-like helper methods
  • Support to view layouts and partials
  • It is released under the MIT, a nice permissive license
